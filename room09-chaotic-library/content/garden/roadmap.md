## 32-bit cafe ideas
<https://32bit.cafe/websiteideas/>

> - Make an /about page  
> - Make a [/uses](https://uses.tech/) page
>   - You can use this for your favourite programs, hardware, art equipment, guitars, stationary, tools... anything you use!
> - Make a credits page with all the tools, tutorials, and resources you used to make your site   
> - Make a [/feeds](https://marcus.io/blog/making-rss-more-visible-again-with-slash-feeds) page
>   - If you have multiple RSS feeds, people can find them all in one place! For example, one for your blog, one for any social media, etc.      
> - Create a page to display old versions of your website design (if it's been around a while)
> - Make a [Webgarden](https://webgardens.neocities.org/)
> - Make a custom 404 page on your site
> - Add the correct [heading information](https://usability.yale.edu/web-accessibility/articles/headings) and tags to all your pages
> - Make a [88x31](https://hekate2.github.io/buttonmaker/) button so people can to link to you on button walls
> - Replace Google Fonts with [self-hosted fonts or a CDN replacement](https://discuss.32bit.cafe/wiki/resources#typefaces-fonts)
> - Make your site's layout responsive for mobile visitors
> - Check how much your site costs to load
> - Check if your site is [accessible for screen readers](https://discuss.32bit.cafe/wiki/resources#accessibility)
> - Make a light mode and dark mode for your site

## James' Coffee Blog ideas
<https://jamesg.blog/2024/02/19/personal-website-ideas/>

> - Make a [blogroll](https://indieweb.org/blogroll) that links to personal websites you like.
> - Make a [guestbook](https://indieweb.org/guestbook) where people can leave notes saying they liked your website.
> - Use WAVE to find potential accessibility issues on different pages on your website.
> - Use PageSpeed Insights to check the speed of your website. Try to make pages load faster if they currently load slow.
> - Add a [print stylesheet](https://petermolnar.net/article/how-to-make-a-print-css/).
> - Make sure your heading sizes are not too close together.
> - Use the [lite-youtube](https://www.zachleat.com/web/youtube-embeds/) web component to improve page loading speeds when you have embedded a YouTube video on the page.
> - Create a style page that shows how every HTML element you use to write content appears. Use this as a reference as you tweak content styles.
> - Add [Webmention](https://indieweb.org/Webmention) support to your personal website so you can receive notifications when people whose sites support Webmention (like this one!) link to or write a post in response to a blog post on your website.
> - Add [sidenotes](https://gwern.net/sidenote) to a blog post.
> - Add [hovercards](https://indieweb.org/hovercard).
